How does it work?

Sign up, select your pick up point and that's it!
There are car pools available that traverse almost all the main routes in the city. Hop into one that's most feasible for you. It's that simple!

Simple. Secure. Reliable.

1

Hitching a Ride

Sign up using your email-id and choose your preferred route and pick-up point. A map of the route for each car pool makes your work easier.

2

Creating a Car Pool

If you own a car you can create a car pool and thus be an owner of it. Advertising your route is made simple by the help of Google Maps. Also, you can choose your car pool members based on your prefernces.

3

Security

Every car pool owner have to be students of PES University. This is done by authenticating the personal information provided during the time of Signing up.
