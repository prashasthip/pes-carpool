Who We Are

A group of enthusiastic students from PES University who came together on a project with a motto to address the most frequent problems faced by students like them and thereby creating "hopIn".

hopIn is evolving the way the student community moves.By seamlessly connecting commuting students through our technology, we make commuting easier, safer and reliable.

We here at hopIn make impossible as possible through hardwork as it can be really fun and that success is more achievable with a talented team that is highly commutable .The problems faced by daily bus commuters are many. From unreliable bus timings, uncomfortable journeys and unsafe travel. hopIn is a platform that would make life easier for such students.
